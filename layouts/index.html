Nexus, SJCET was formed on 12 September, 2018 by a group of students to support and organize Free/Libre and Open-Source Software and Hardware related activities in SJCET, Palai. We believe in great ideas, aims to create awareness on software and hardware freedom and to promote its usage among the student community through both technical and non-technical programmes. It is a unique platform for students and technologists to share their technical and professional knowledge for the benefit of the society.

We Support:

The freedom of

Speech, Press, Association on the Internet

The Right to

use, study, copy, modify, and redistribute computer programs

The Right to

study, modify, distribute, make, and sell the design or hardware based on that design.

The Right to

write software and engineer hardware unimpeded by private monopolies
